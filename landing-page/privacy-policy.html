Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Blue Video and OnlyBL collect, use, store, and share information when you use the app, website, and support channels.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as email address, username, and profile details you provide.
  • Device and log information such as app version, device type, language, IP address, and diagnostic events.
  • Usage information such as settings selections, support requests, and in-app activity needed to operate the service.
  • Transaction-related information when purchases, subscriptions, or payment verification are processed.

How We Use Information

  • Provide app features, account access, content delivery, and customer support.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, troubleshoot issues, and improve app stability.
  • Send service-related notices such as login verification, policy updates, and important account messages.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our platform rules.

How Information May Be Shared

  • With infrastructure or service providers that help operate hosting, storage, analytics, notifications, or payments.
  • When required by law, regulation, court order, or valid government request.
  • To protect users, the platform, and our rights in connection with fraud, abuse, or security incidents.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain records, resolve disputes, meet legal obligations, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and the reason it was collected.

Your Choices

  • You can review and update certain profile information inside the app.
  • You can contact support if you need help with account access or privacy-related requests.
  • You can stop using the service at any time, but some records may still be retained where required.

Children

Blue Video is intended only for adults. The service is not directed to children, and we do not intend to knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Contact

For privacy questions or support requests, contact x.com/onlyblcom or use App Support inside the service when available.
